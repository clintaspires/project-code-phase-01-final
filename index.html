About

CASio K3YS is a NYC based DJ, Producer, Singer-Songwriter. He has been part of the NYC underground music scene since 2004 when he began performing in the music group DaLipstyxx under the stage name Ashlee along with fellow band members Mimi Valdez, Milan from RuPaul's Drag Race fame and LT from Bravo's Pregnant in Hells. In 2007, under the stage name AC he left with LT to form the new electronic, dance duo THE KIKI TWINS. He co-wrote, produced and performed all of the groups catalog in addition to working with German-based DJ Kris Menace on various projects. Along the way he hedged this experience into a side-project career as a club DJ in NYC's colorful bar scene at such venues like Hell's Kitchen's Industry and Hardware Bars as well as Chelsea's Barracuda Bar and Elmo Restaurant to name a few.
